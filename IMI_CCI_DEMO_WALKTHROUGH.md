# IMI CCI-Style Executive Demo Walkthrough

Demo positioning: WinsProposal helps severe-service valve companies transform proposal engineering into reusable revenue intelligence.

This is an IMI CCI-style demo account only. It is not an official IMI implementation and does not use IMI trademarks as customer claims.

## 2-Minute Executive Opening

WinsProposal is an Industrial Revenue Intelligence Platform focused on complex engineered bids. For severe-service control valve companies, the strongest use case is engineering proposal intelligence: reducing proposal engineering workload, preserving application knowledge, accelerating bid response, and improving compliance and TBE quality.

The demo shows how a complex RFP moves from upload to extracted requirements, reusable Knowledge Vault content, proposal-stage engineering intelligence, TBE responses, technical visuals, executive KPI reporting, and professional PDF/DOCX exports.

## Management Pain Points

- Proposal engineering overload during high-value bid cycles.
- Dependency on senior experts for repeated severe-service narratives.
- Repeated technical responses for anti-surge, cavitation, noise, NACE, hydrogen, and steam conditioning topics.
- Manual compliance effort and missed mandatory clauses.
- Technical bid evaluation complexity across materials, trim, testing, inspection, deviations, and documentation.
- Knowledge trapped in past proposals, datasheets, and individual engineer memory.
- Long turnaround time for complex valve packages.

## Demo Storyline

1. Upload a severe-service RFP.
2. Extract engineering, commercial, inspection, compliance, and delivery requirements.
3. Reuse approved Knowledge Vault content for severe-service valve narratives.
4. Generate proposal-stage engineering intelligence with clear validation disclaimers.
5. Create TBE responses for materials, trim, pressure class, actuator, accessories, testing, inspection, documentation, deviations, and engineering comments.
6. Show technical visualizations and drawings for value chain, engineering dependency, calculation workflow, compliance, risks, and application topology.
7. Show executive KPI dashboard metrics for bid value, turnaround reduction, reuse, compliance, hours saved, TBE completion, approval time, risk, and win score.
8. Export professional PDF and DOCX packs with distinct section-specific visual layouts.
9. Discuss pilot outcomes and implementation path.

## Persona Talking Points

VP Business Development: More bid throughput, faster management visibility, clearer win themes, and less dependency on last-minute engineering escalation.

Proposal Director: A controlled workflow for RFP extraction, reusable technical content, compliance closure, TBE completion, and export readiness.

Engineering Manager: Proposal-stage intelligence that preserves severe-service expertise while clearly requiring qualified final sizing and design validation.

Application Engineer: Faster starting point for process condition review, severe-service risk flags, trim narratives, actuator/accessory notes, and standards awareness.

Compliance Lead: Structured compliance matrix, deviation handling, evidence tracking, inspection/testing clauses, and documentation readiness.

## ROI Discussion

- Proposal cycle time reduction: target 40-60%.
- Engineering effort saved: target 25-40 hours per complex bid.
- Proposal reuse: target 50-70% reusable engineering content.
- Compliance acceleration: target 90%+ coverage before final review.
- Improved bid throughput through faster TBE and approval cycles.
