# Engineering Proposal Intelligence for Severe-Service Control Valve Companies

This is an IMI CCI-style demo concept only, not an official IMI implementation.

## Current Proposal Challenges

Severe-service valve bids require expert interpretation of pressure drop, cavitation, flashing, choked flow, noise, vibration, materials, NACE applicability, hydrogen compatibility, steam conditioning, inspection, testing, and deviations. Much of this knowledge is reused manually across bids, creating high workload and inconsistent proposal quality.

## WinsProposal Solution

WinsProposal turns proposal engineering into reusable revenue intelligence. It extracts RFP requirements, organizes engineering and compliance obligations, reuses approved Knowledge Vault content, generates proposal-stage engineering intelligence, drafts TBE responses, visualizes technical workflows, and exports management-ready PDF/DOCX proposal packs.

## Why Severe-Service Valves Need Specialized Proposal Intelligence

Generic AI proposal writing is not enough. Severe-service valve proposals need structured engineering context, application-specific risk flags, standards awareness, qualification disclaimers, deviation control, inspection evidence, and review workflows before a bid can be released confidently.
