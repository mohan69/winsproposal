# IMI CCI-Style 30-Minute Meeting Agenda

Meeting purpose: Evaluate a focused WinsProposal severe-service control valve proposal intelligence pilot.  
Audience: Senior management, business development, proposal leadership, application engineering, compliance.

---

## 0:00-5:00 Context

- Confirm the objective: improve proposal speed, engineering leverage, compliance coverage, and management visibility.
- Align on current proposal challenges for severe-service control valve bids.
- Clarify that WinsProposal supports engineering review and internal tools; it does not replace them.
- Frame the session as a practical pilot discussion, not a broad system implementation.

Expected output: Shared understanding of the business problem and the demo lens.

---

## 5:00-15:00 10-Minute Demo

- RFP upload and opportunity context.
- Requirement extraction and source traceability.
- Knowledge Vault reuse for approved technical and proposal content.
- Proposal-stage engineering intelligence: risks, assumptions, clarifications.
- Compliance/TBE workflow and ownership tracking.
- Technical visuals and proposal export.
- Executive KPI dashboard.

Expected output: Management sees the end-to-end severe-service proposal workflow.

---

## 15:00-25:00 Discussion

- Which current bottlenecks are most painful: engineering time, compliance effort, TBE response, approvals, or proposal formatting?
- Which severe-service proposal types would be best for a pilot?
- What historical proposal content can be safely used in the Knowledge Vault?
- What controls are required for engineering review, technical accuracy, and data security?
- Which pilot KPIs would senior management consider meaningful?

Expected output: Agreement on priority use cases, constraints, and success metrics.

---

## 25:00-30:00 Pilot Next Steps

- Select 2-3 representative severe-service RFPs.
- Nominate proposal, engineering, compliance, and executive owners.
- Confirm baseline metrics and target improvements.
- Agree pilot timeline, review cadence, and final management review date.
- Confirm whether to proceed with a 30-day pilot scoping call.

Expected output: Named owners, pilot candidate bids, and a clear go/no-go next step.
