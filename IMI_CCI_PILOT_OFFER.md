# Severe-Service Proposal Intelligence Pilot

This is an IMI CCI-style commercial pilot outline only, not an official IMI proposal.

## Duration

30 days.

## Scope

- 1 business unit.
- 1 proposal team.
- 5-10 complex RFPs.
- 100-300 historical proposal and technical documents.
- Knowledge Vault setup for severe-service valve narratives.
- Compliance and TBE automation.
- Proposal-stage engineering intelligence sections.
- Diagram and visual generation.
- Executive dashboard for bid value, turnaround, reuse, compliance, hours saved, TBE completion, approval time, risk, and win probability.

## Success Metrics

- Proposal turnaround reduction.
- Engineering hours saved per complex bid.
- Proposal reuse percentage.
- Compliance coverage percentage.
- TBE completion time.
- Approval cycle time.

## Commercial Framing

Pilot fee placeholder: Rs. 3L-8L depending on document volume, workflow scope, integrations, and executive reporting depth.

## Governance

All engineering intelligence is proposal-stage only. Final sizing and design must be validated by qualified engineers using company-approved tools and standards.
