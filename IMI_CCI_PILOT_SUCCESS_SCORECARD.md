# IMI CCI-Style Pilot Success Scorecard

Pilot: 30-day severe-service proposal intelligence pilot  
Scope: 2-3 representative severe-service control valve RFPs  
Purpose: Measure whether WinsProposal improves proposal turnaround, engineering leverage, compliance readiness, and executive visibility.

| Metric | Baseline Metric | Target Improvement | Measurement Method | Responsible Owner | Pilot Review Cadence |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|---|
| Proposal turnaround reduction | Current average days from RFP receipt to proposal-ready draft | 20-30% reduction | Compare baseline proposals against pilot proposal timestamps | Proposal Director | Weekly and final review |
| Engineering hours saved | Current engineering hours per complex severe-service proposal | 15-25% reduction | Track engineering review hours, repeated questions, and rework hours | Engineering Manager | Weekly |
| Proposal reuse % | Current percentage of proposal content reused from approved historical material | 30-50% approved reuse where applicable | Measure sections sourced from Knowledge Vault and accepted after review | Proposal Lead | Weekly |
| Compliance coverage | Current percentage of requirements explicitly addressed before final review | 95%+ mapped coverage before management review | Compliance matrix status and source traceability audit | Compliance Lead | Twice weekly |
| TBE completion time | Current hours or days to complete technical bid evaluation response | 20-30% reduction | Track TBE item creation, assignment, response, and approval timestamps | Application Engineering Lead | Weekly |
| Approval cycle time | Current time from first management review to final approval | 15-25% reduction | Track approval request date, open comments, rework cycles, and final sign-off | Proposal Director | Weekly |
| Proposal throughput | Current number of proposals completed per month with current team capacity | 10-20% throughput improvement | Compare completed proposal count and backlog movement | VP Business Development | Final review |
| Executive visibility | Current visibility into status, risk, compliance, and approvals | Dashboard available for all pilot bids | Management dashboard review and stakeholder feedback | Executive Sponsor | Weekly and final review |

---

## Review Questions

- Did the pilot reduce time spent reading and structuring RFP requirements?
- Did application engineering receive better summaries and fewer repetitive questions?
- Did the Knowledge Vault improve reuse without weakening technical control?
- Did compliance and TBE tracking reduce late-stage gaps?
- Did management gain earlier visibility into proposal readiness and risk?
- Which workflow steps should be configured before a broader rollout?

---

## Pilot Decision Criteria

Continue or expand the pilot if:

- At least four target metrics show measurable improvement.
- Engineering confirms the system supports, rather than bypasses, technical review.
- Proposal leadership sees reduced manual coordination effort.
- Compliance coverage becomes easier to audit before submission.
- Management receives clearer bid readiness visibility than the current process provides.
