## How To Use The Pack

- Use the executive presentation as the management conversation backbone.
- Use the 10-minute demo script during the live product walkthrough so the founder can focus on listening and relationship-building.
- Use discovery questions before or after the demo to identify the strongest pilot business case.
- Use the outreach messages to open the conversation without sounding overly sales-driven.
- Use the scorecard to keep the pilot discussion measurable and management-friendly.
- Use objection handling as preparation before senior stakeholder meetings.

---

## Recommended Meeting Flow

1. Open with the 30-minute meeting agenda.
2. Spend 5 minutes confirming the proposal workflow problem and business context.
3. Run the 10-minute demo script without over-explaining product features.
4. Use discovery questions to uncover current baseline pain: turnaround time, engineering effort, TBE workload, compliance gaps, approval delays, and bid visibility.
5. Introduce the pilot success scorecard as the ROI mechanism.
6. Close with a specific pilot ask: 2-3 representative RFPs, named proposal/engineering/compliance owners, and a 30-day review cadence.
