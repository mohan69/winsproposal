# Severe-Service Control Valve Landing Page

## Page Route

`/solutions/severe-service-control-valves`

## Target Audience

- VP Business Development
- Proposal Director
- Engineering Manager
- Application Engineering
- Compliance / Documentation Lead

## Messaging Strategy

The page positions WinsProposal as a generic, professional proposal intelligence platform for severe-service control valve companies. It focuses on management-level outcomes: reduced proposal engineering workload, higher reuse of approved application knowledge, faster bid response, improved compliance/TBE quality, and executive visibility into proposal execution.

The page intentionally avoids customer logos, trademarks, partnership claims, or any wording that presents the experience as an official page for a specific valve manufacturer. The tone is account-based and enterprise-grade, suitable for direct outreach and executive walkthroughs.
