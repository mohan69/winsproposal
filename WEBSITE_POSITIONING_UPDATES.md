- Repositioned the hero around "Industrial Revenue Intelligence Platform" for executive buyers.

  - proposal throughput
  - compliance quality
  - engineering productivity
  - reusable bid intelligence
  - operational visibility
- Added a homepage KPI strip with faster turnaround, 2-3x throughput, better compliance coverage, reduced engineering effort, and reusable bid knowledge.
- Reframed the before/after story from "generic AI vs. WinsProposal" to a clearer operational transformation:
  - Excel compliance sheets to AI-generated compliance matrices
  - copy-paste proposals to reusable proposal intelligence
  - disconnected engineering responses to AI-assisted technical visuals
  - manual bid/no-bid decisions to executive bid dashboards and measurable KPIs
- Added "AI-Assisted Technical Visualization" as a dedicated enterprise moat without exposing implementation terminology.
- Added "Enterprise Knowledge Preservation" to position the Knowledge Vault as a durable company asset, not just document storage.
- Added executive dashboard visuals for pipeline, bid value, turnaround, compliance coverage, reuse, and engineering hours saved.
- Preserved industrial specificity across EPC, valves, pumps, APIs, TBE workflows, Go/No-Go scoring, diagram generation, proposal examples, and demo CTAs.
